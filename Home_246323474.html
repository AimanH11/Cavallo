About Us

Cavallo Import Export is also a premier importer and exporter of quality food products, based in India. They specialize in importing and exporting products such as pastas, pasta sauces, olives, olive oils, tomatoes, coffee, industrial coffee machines, yogurt, vinegars, gelato ingredients, and machines.
Cavallo Import Export also distributes to General Trade, Hypermarkets, Institutions & Horeca segments, within India, Sri Lanka, Indonesia, Maldives, Bangladesh & Nepal.In terms of exports, Cavallo Import Export exports Indian origin edible oils, spices, fresh & frozen fruits, vegetables, and offers organic & conventional produce with customized packing. They are also committed to importing and exporting only the best quality food products across the globe, and are quickly emerging as one of the leading importers in India.
